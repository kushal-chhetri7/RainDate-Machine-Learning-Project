What is RainDate?

Raindate is a website which predicts the amount of rainfall on a particular month of a year both in past and future using machine learning algorithm. We have collected weather data of twenty years from NCHM National Center for Hydrology and Meteorology and fed it to our system to predict the amount of rainfall.

{% comment %} More {% endcomment %}

What should you Do?

We have translated a simple idea into an exotic website that helps in predicting the amount of rainfall. To get started with prediction, you just have to go to the form section and fill out the details like temprature maximum, temprature minimum, relative humidity, windspeed, date and location. The system will then predict the expected rainfall with the provided data.

How accurate our Prediction is?

Our model has 78% score in train and has 76% score in test. Test data provides a final, real-world check of an unseen dataset to confirm that the ML algorithm was trained effectively. That means that the data is 76% correct for any unseen data that you provide.
